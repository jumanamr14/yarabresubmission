Main ICU specialties

Critical Care Medicine (Intensivists):
- Doctors specially trained to manage critically ill patients in the ICU.
-Focus on multi-organ failure, ventilation, and life support systems.
Anesthesiology:
- Provides expertise in pain management, sedation, and mechanical ventilation.
Pulmonology:
- Manages severe respiratory conditions, such as acute respiratory distress syndrome (ARDS) or chronic lung diseases requiring mechanical ventilation.
